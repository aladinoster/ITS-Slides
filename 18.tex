    \begin{block}{Demand \& Supply paradigm}
    For a road $r$ we define
    \begin{itemize}
    \item Demand of $r$ the flow of vehicles that can go out from $r$
    \item Supply of $r$ the flow of vehicles that $r$ can receive
    \end{itemize}
    \end{block}
